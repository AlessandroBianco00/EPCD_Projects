Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your tasted buds with this festive focaccia topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will became your new favourite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) of all–purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1 1/4 cup extra vergin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1–2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing th top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it’s not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Making the focaccia dough
In a medium–sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absordbed.
